You are given and array of N integers. 
You are supposed to return the maximum sum of the subsequence with the constraint that no two elements are adjacent in the array.

1 <= T <= 500
1 <= N <= 1000
0 <= ARR[i] <= 10^5
Where 'ARR[i]' denotes the 'i-th' element in the array/list.
Time Limit: 1 sec.

Sample Input 1:
2
3
1 2 4
4
2149
Sample Output 1:
5
11
